Master the art of balancing autonomy and accountability

Module 1: Autonomy vs Accountability

5 Steps

Learn how to navigate challenging conversations with team members who value autonomy while maintaining accountability for outcomes.

What You'll Learn:

  • Recognition: Identify defensive responses and underlying concerns
  • Transition: Move from reactive to strategic communication
  • Production: Craft effective responses in high-stakes situations

Meet Alex:

Throughout this module, you'll interact with Alex, a skilled professional who is:

  • Autonomy-driven and values independence
  • Defensive when feeling micromanaged
  • Responsive to clarity and choice

How It Works:

  1. Recognition Steps (1-3): Choose the best response from multiple options
  2. Transition Step (4): Options are provided but may not be ideal - you can craft your own
  3. Production Step (5): Demonstrate mastery by crafting your own response

Note: If you struggle with a step, the system will provide personalized guidance and practice opportunities.
